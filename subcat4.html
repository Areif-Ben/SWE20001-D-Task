Pickup Truck

  • Colorado

    Colorado is designed to help you speed up work, deliver more fun and always look good doing it. With a fully boxed frame and 4-wheel disc brakes with Duralife® brake rotors, it’s built for durability. When it comes to utility, there’s a CornerStep rear bumper, corner hand grips and tons of cargo tie-downs.

    When you’re putting together your team, you need workers who are strong, smart and capable of doing the job you put in front of them all day long. In other words, you need Silverado – built on the legacy of the most dependable, longest-lasting full-size pickups on the road.
